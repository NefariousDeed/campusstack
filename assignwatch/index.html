Features

Everything you need to stay on top of your coursework

⏱
Live Deadline Countdowns
Assignments due within 12 hours show a live countdown timer so you always know what's most urgent.
🎨
Per-Course Color Coding
Each course gets its own color. Spot CHEM 101 vs CS 201 at a glance without reading a word.
📌
Pin High-Priority Work
Pin any assignment to the top regardless of due date. Great for projects you need to chip away at over time.
✅
Manual Mark Complete
Mark assignments done directly in the popup. The badge count only shows what's left.
📅
Google Calendar Export
One click to add any assignment to your Google Calendar with the correct due date pre-filled.
🔒
Fully Local & Private
Your Canvas API token never leaves your device. No servers, no tracking, no accounts.
How It Works

Set up in under a minute

1
Install from Chrome Web Store
Click "Add to Chrome" and confirm the extension install. No account needed.
2
Open Canvas and grab your token
Go to your Canvas account settings, generate an access token, and copy it.
3
Paste into AssignWatch
Open the extension popup, go to Settings, paste your token. AssignWatch connects instantly.
4
Stay on top of everything
Your assignments appear automatically, sorted by urgency. Overdue items are highlighted in red.
🔒
Your data stays on your device
AssignWatch stores your Canvas token locally using Chrome's secure storage API. The token is only sent directly to your Canvas instance - never to our servers (we don't have any). Zero analytics, zero tracking.
