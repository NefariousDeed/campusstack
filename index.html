About

Built by a student, for students.

Campus Stack is a solo project - every extension is built to solve a real pain point in the college browser experience, from missing assignment deadlines to picking the wrong professor during registration.

Everything is free, privacy-minded, and designed to fit directly into the websites students already use every day.
